RECORDING STUDIO, CUMBRIA, THE LAKE DISTRICT

Massif Music is a fully equipped recording studio based in the heart of the Lake District, on the outskirts of Keswick.

We can cater for your audio needs – recording, mixing, mastering - no project is too big or small.

Established in 2013 we have worked with musicians of all backgrounds and styles. We specialise in taking projects from the ideas stage to full release. Take a listen to our portfolio to hear examples of some of the artists we have worked with over the years.
